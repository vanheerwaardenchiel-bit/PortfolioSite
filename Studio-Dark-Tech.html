Overview

This semester I participated in Studio Dark Tech - Doppen, a project focused on creating ditigal interventions to create awareness about use of headphones/noise cancelling in public spaces. Our product owner is Gemeente Amsterdam, led under Yuri Westplat. This page contains several prototypes and documents related to digital interventions, research and other supporting material created during this semester.
